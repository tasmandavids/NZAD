The idea

Every studio runs on rhythm. Olune keeps it.

Classes by the hour. Memberships by the month. Terms by the season. The name carries its own moon — and the moon is the cycle a studio lives by. Olune brings the whole orbit of a studio into one place: the schedule, the people, the payments. Less time on admin, more time in the room.

Calm

Quiet by default. Nothing shouts, nothing rushes.

Considered

Every screen earns its place. We remove before we add.

Warm

Made for people, named by name. Software with a pulse.

Precise

Numbers add up. Times line up. Trust is in the detail.

The mark

A waxing moon

The symbol is the moon that gives Olune its name — the “o” in olune, drawn as a waxing moon. It’s built from a single disc and its shadow: nothing more. The dark side is always Midnight, the lit side always Moonlight. It reads at any size, from a favicon to a foyer sign.

One circle, one shadow Dark side · Midnight Lit side · Moonlight gradient No outline · no inner detail

How Olune speaks

Like a calm colleague who has done this a thousand times. Plain words, no panic, never a wall of exclamation marks. The interface should feel like it’s on your side.
