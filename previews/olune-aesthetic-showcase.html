Studio policies

Trial classes are complimentary and run for the full session length. Please arrive ten minutes early so our front desk can settle your dancer in.

Term fees are billed in advance and may be paid by auto-pay. Costumes for the end-of-year showcase are invoiced separately in term three.

Absences reported more than 24 hours ahead may be credited toward a make-up class, subject to availability in an equivalent level.

Our studios are a phones-down, encouragement-up space. We celebrate effort over outcome and progress over perfection.

Withdrawals require two weeks’ written notice. Medical holds are available on request with a supporting note.
